How iCal Works

Why we need your iCal link

Your iCal feed is what powers the date search on our rentals page. It lets us show whether your unit or lot is available when guests pick check-in and check-out dates.

Without an iCal link, we can still show your card, but we cannot surface accurate availability status in search results.

Airbnb: find your iCal link

  1. Open your listing in Airbnb host mode.
  2. Go to Pricing and availability then Calendar sync.
  3. Copy the Export calendar (iCal) link and paste it into this form.

VRBO: find your iCal link

  1. Open your listing calendar in VRBO owner dashboard.
  2. Go to Import/Export or Calendar sync.
  3. Copy your Export iCal URL and paste it into this form.
