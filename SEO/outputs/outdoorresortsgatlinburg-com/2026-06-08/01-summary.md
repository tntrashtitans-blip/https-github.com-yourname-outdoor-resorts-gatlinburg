# Outdoor Resorts Gatlinburg SEO Audit Summary

Date: 2026-06-08

## Executive Summary

The site is in a strong technical and local-content position for a static local SEO build. The main public pages have unique titles, meta descriptions, canonical URLs, single H1s, Open Graph images, crawlable internal links, and valid JSON-LD where schema is present. Production is live over HTTPS, and the homepage, rentals page, robots file, and sitemap all returned HTTP 200 from Netlify during this audit.

Today's Browse Units additions are also live: Lots 122, 206, and 9 appear in the production `listings.js`, and all three new Airbnb cover images return HTTP 200.

## Where Things Stand

- Technical crawlability: Good.
- Indexing basics: Good, pending Search Console verification.
- Local SEO page intent: Good.
- Internal linking: Good across the main guest and investor journeys.
- Schema: Good foundation, but incomplete on a few money pages.
- Rental page freshness: Strong after adding the three new calendar-connected listings.
- Measurement visibility: Weak because GSC, GA4, GBP, and competitor/ranking data are not available in the workspace.
