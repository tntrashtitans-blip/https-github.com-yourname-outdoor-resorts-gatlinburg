Resort Information

10 minutes from Gatlinburg. A world away.

Outdoor Resorts Gatlinburg sits on East Parkway about 10 minutes from downtown Gatlinburg, close enough for restaurants, attractions, Dollywood, and mountain-town energy, with the relaxed RV campground setting people come to the Smokies to find.

RV rentals and RV lots Rent a ready-to-stay RV or bring your own camper to a privately owned lot.
