Rental FAQ

Questions guests ask before booking

Are these rentals booked through Outdoor Resorts Gatlinburg?

No. Listings are individually owned and are booked through the owner's Airbnb, VRBO, Hipcamp, or owner-direct page. This site is an independent rental directory for comparing verified ORG listings.

What is the difference between an RV rental and a lot rental?

An RV rental or park model rental includes the unit. A lot rental is for guests bringing their own RV or camper, so confirm lot size, hookups, parking, and access before booking.

Does the date search show every available listing?

No. Date search checks connected iCal feeds only. Some listings may still be available even if their calendar is not connected, so always confirm on the booking platform.

Can I tent camp at Outdoor Resorts Gatlinburg?

No. Tent camping is not offered. The directory is focused on RV rentals, park model rentals, and rentable RV lots for guests bringing their own camper.
